Three College Park council members are running for mayor. Here’s a look at their past votes
  • I pitched this story idea, a breakdown of how three mayoral candidates voted during their city council tenures, in the midst of campaigns for a special election. The city was reeling with the arrest of the mayor, and now three council members were vying for the same seat. At this point, I had reported on the College Park City Council for nearly 1.5 years and wanted to put my knowledge of each council member out for the voters. As I was watching each candidate’s electioneering, I noticed that few mentioned specific council votes and I wanted to give voters an accurate depiction of how candidates really vote. I chose these four votes due to their recency and because they are all split votes - a rare occurrence in the College Park City Council.

'A story worth telling': Refugee Welcome Network helps Afghans settle in Hagerstown area
  • For this story, published A1 in The Herald-Mail, I featured a little-known group in Hagerstown helping Afghanistan refugees.
