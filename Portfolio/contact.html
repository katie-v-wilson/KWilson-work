About me

My name is Katharine Wilson and I'm an aspiring political reporter. I have experience in covering local politics, multimedia projects and breaking news reporting. I'm a junior journalism and government & politics major at the University of Maryland.

I have covered the College Park City Council since January 2022 through an internship with College Park Here & Now and currently for The Diamondback. During these expereinces I have covered city redistricting conflicts, the mayor's arrest, environmental legislation, and elections.

Aside from writing I have experiences with multimedia reporting including radio broadcasting through WMUC radio, photo and video reporting expereince through my coursework, and data journalism skills.

This summer, I'll be a summer reporting intern for Herald-Mail Media through the Local News Network.
